Our Mining Solutions Services

At Rock Hill Mining Solutions, we provide a wide range of innovative and reliable services tailored to meet the unique needs of the mining industry:

Mining Technology Services

We offer advanced mining technology solutions, including:

  • Condition Monitoring: Real-time monitoring of equipment and processes to ensure optimal performance.
  • Personnel & Equipment Tracking: Systems to track personnel and equipment, improving safety and operational efficiency.
  • Surface Mining Technology: Real-time tracking of excavators and dump trucks to enhance operational efficiency.
  • Ore Tracking Devices: Monitors ore dumping activities for accurate material tracking in underground and surface mining operations.
  • Control Room Setup: Designing and installing centralized control rooms for efficient monitoring and management of mining operations.

Ground Support Solutions

We provide comprehensive ground support solutions, including:

  • Rock Bolts: Rebar Bolts, Cable Bolts, Self-Drilling Bolts (SDBs), Spin-Lock Bolts, Friction Bolts (Split Sets).
  • Roof and Rib Supports: Canopy Roof Supports, Standing Roof Supports, Mesh and Straps.
  • Resin and Grout Products: Resin Cartridges for anchoring bolts, Pumpable Grouts for filling cavities and stabilizing ground.
  • Mine Mesh Systems: High-tensile steel mesh and lightweight polymer grids for added safety.
  • Rock Monitoring Systems: Electronic monitoring of rock mass movement.
  • Paste Fill Binder: Cost-effective super strength paste binder solutions.

Ventilation Solutions

Our ventilation solutions include:

  • Ventilation Fans: Efficient and reliable single & twin-staged secondary ventilation fans.
  • Ventilation Control Devices: Flexible, rigid, and automated ventilation ducting and regulators.
  • Ventilation Instrumentation: Modern instruments with real-time monitoring capabilities.
  • Mine Ventilation Support Services: Supply and installation of automated ventilation doors and regulators.
  • Local Exhaust Ventilation: Industrial ventilation solution design, supply, and installation.

Mine Fire Management Systems

We provide fire management systems tailored to mining environments, including:

  • Fire Engineering & Modeling: Designing fire prevention systems.
  • Fire Suppression Installations: Installing hydrant and suppression systems.
  • Emergency Evacuation Design & Servicing: Developing evacuation plans and providing servicing.
  • Refuge Chambers: Supply of refuge chambers and self-rescuers.

Procurement & Logistics

Our procurement and logistics services include:

  • Mining Supplies: Essential mining equipment and materials.
  • Supply of Safety Equipment: High-quality PPE, gas detectors, and monitors.
  • Agency Arrangements: Partnering with global manufacturers for efficient supply services.
  • Logistics Services: Managing logistics from ports to mine sites across West Africa.

Training Services

We offer comprehensive training services, including:

  • Basic to Advanced Mining Software Training.
  • Specialized Technology Training: Hands-on training in advanced mining technologies.
  • Mining Courses & Certifications: Certifications and courses to enhance workforce skillsets.

Industrial Hygiene Services

Our industrial hygiene services include:

  • Sample Preparation: Ensuring accurate analysis by preparing samples for testing.
  • Dust & Diesel Particulate Matter (DPM) Analysis: Monitoring airborne particulates for safety and compliance.
  • Silica & Metal Fumes Analysis: Testing for harmful substances to ensure worker safety.
